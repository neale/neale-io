Windows is a very simple collection to make once the backbones for all the other collections is in place. Its much the same process as HyperEvolution or Digital Flow, but with an added dimension.

Individual pieces from HyperEvolution or Digital Flow can be thought of as views into the internals of a massively high dimensional neural network. Like 2D slices of an otherwise incomprehensible landscape. The pieces that make up Windows, are like 3D volumes that have been cut from this landscape. The curves and shapes made by undulating surfaces seen in Windows, are a sort of self-portrait of an AI.

There's a lot of AI art out there right now, and the creativity expressed by these tools together with their users, is only going to grow. While you can even ask these AI artists to create something like a self-portrait, Windows is a true picture of their internals, rather than just a projection of self.
