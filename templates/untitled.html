This series remains untitled.

Untitled is the heaviest collection so far in terms of compute and post-processing. I used the same generation pipeline as DigitalFlow, but added an addtional stage at the end. I had been experimenting with Perlin and Simplex noise as a way of breathing a little more life into my pieces. The art I usually generate is sharp and can feel extremely loud. I wanted to make something softer, art that could take up a full wall without dominating the space. I found that Perlin and Simplex noise added a more natural feel when used as forward mapping transform (warping). Untitled is the result of that process after some color grading and cleaning of spurious noisey components.
